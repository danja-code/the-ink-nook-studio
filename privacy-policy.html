Privacy Policy – The Ink Nook Studio

Last updated: April 2026

The Ink Nook Studio ("we", "our") operates an automated Pinterest publishing tool for our Etsy shop. This policy explains how we handle data.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from Pinterest users. Our application only accesses our own Pinterest account to create and schedule pins linking to our Etsy shop listings.

Third-Party Services

We use the Pinterest API solely to publish content to our own account. We use the Etsy platform to sell digital products. Neither service shares user data with us through our automation.
